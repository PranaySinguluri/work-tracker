How to share your calendar with friends
  1. Go to calendar.google.com
  2. Find your Class & Work calendar in the left sidebar
  3. Click the three dots → Settings and sharing
  4. Under "Share with specific people" → Add their email
  5. Choose permission: View only or Make changes
  6. They'll get an email invite — once accepted, they see your events
